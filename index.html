INSPIRATION

Sometime around 2003, I acquired Ratchet & Clank: Going Commando for the Playstation 2, which to this day I still cite as my favourite game of all time. I could talk for hours on end about my love for the game, but for the purposes of this page I wish to talk about a hidden location within the game known as the Insomniac Museum.

There are multiple ways to reach this location: fully upgrading every weapon, using a teleporter on Planet Boldan which is only active between 3AM - 4AM, and (my personal favourite) the "power line exploit" also found on Planet Boldan. Upon reaching the museum, the player is free to explore and browse the various exhibits showcasing unused material alongside developer commentaries, which include:

  • Developement resources - Notably a large testing area for the player's movement
  • Feature demonstrations - Particle effects, breakable meshes and gravity surfaces to name a few
  • Scrapped content - Models for weapons and enemies which were ultimately removed
This look behind the curtain, showcasing aspects of development which would usually be kept within the studio's walls and explaining what made the game tick, left a lasting impression which inspires me to this day.
