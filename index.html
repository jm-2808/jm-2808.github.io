Stealing Stuff

01/2020 - 05/2020
"You know what they say about 'if it ain't nailed down', but if it is ... find a claw hammer"

A group project from my final year, Stealing Stuff is an arcade-style game where players sneak around and steal everything that isn't nailed down, working together as a team to steal larger items. I acted as the Lead Programmer for this project, which involved delegating tasks to other programmers on the team

This project achieved 3rd place in both the Sumo Digital Award for Game Design and Rockstar Leeds Award for Best Team categories at the Game Republic Student Showcase 2020
